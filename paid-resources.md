## What Problem This Playbook Solves

Many candidates prepare for Amazon interviews by only solving random LeetCode problems.

That is not enough.

Amazon interviews often test a combination of:

- Coding ability
- Problem-solving clarity
- Communication
- Leadership Principles
- Ownership
- Project depth
- Ability to explain trade-offs
- Ability to handle ambiguity

This playbook helps you prepare for the full interview signal, not just coding.

## Why I Created This

I created this material because many candidates feel confused by the Amazon process.

Common questions include:

- Why does my portal status say “No longer under consideration”?
- Is my interview still active?
- Is this for Amazon Leo or a general SDE role?
- How many coding rounds should I expect?
- How should I prepare for Leadership Principles?
- What kind of project stories should I prepare?
- How should I talk to the recruiter?

This playbook is designed to reduce confusion and give candidates a clear preparation structure.
