
# F-1 / OPT Recruiter Questions

This file is for international students who are interviewing for Amazon SDE roles and want to discuss work authorization professionally.

> Disclaimer: This is not legal or immigration advice. Always confirm with your recruiter, university DSO, and qualified immigration counsel.

---

## Goal

Your goal is to be clear, professional, and accurate.

Do not hide your status.

Do not overexplain.

Do not sound uncertain.

---

## Simple Work Authorization Explanation

Example:

```text
I am currently on F-1 status and eligible for post-completion OPT. My degree is in a STEM field, so 
I may also be eligible for STEM OPT extension, subject to the required process and employer requirements.
```
