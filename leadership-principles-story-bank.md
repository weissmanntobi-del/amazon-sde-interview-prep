# Amazon Leadership Principles Story Bank

Amazon behavioral interviews are deeply connected to Leadership Principles.

Your goal is not to memorize definitions.

Your goal is to prepare real stories that prove how you behave under pressure.

---

## STAR Format

Use this structure for every answer:

### Situation

What was the context?

### Task

What were you responsible for?

### Action

What specific actions did you take?

### Result

What changed because of your actions?

Add numbers whenever possible.

---

## Strong Story Formula

A strong answer should show:

- Real conflict or difficulty
- Your personal responsibility
- Specific technical or human action
- Measurable result
- Reflection or learning

Weak answer:

> We worked as a team and fixed the issue.

Strong answer:

> I discovered that our API latency increased from 300ms to 2.5s after deployment. I owned the investigation, found an inefficient database query, added indexing, improved caching, and reduced p95 latency to 420ms.

---

## Minimum Story Bank

Prepare at least 8–10 stories.

| Story Type               | Leadership Principles              |
|--------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Solved customer pain     | Customer Obsession, Ownership      |
| Fixed production issue   | Dive Deep, Ownership               |
| Improved quality         | Insist on the Highest Standards    |
| Simplified a system      | Invent and Simplify                |
| Disagreed with teammate  | Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit |
| Delivered under deadline | Deliver Results                    |
| Learned new technology   | Learn and Be Curious               |
| Made trade-off decision  | Are Right, A Lot                   |
| Helped teammate          | Hire and Develop the Best          |
| Failed and recovered     | Ownership, Learn and Be Curious    |
