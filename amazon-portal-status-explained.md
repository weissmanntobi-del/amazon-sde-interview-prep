# Amazon Portal Status Explained

## Scenario

You applied for an Amazon SDE role.

You completed the online assessment.

A recruiter contacted you for next steps.

Then the Amazon portal showed:

> No longer under consideration

But you still received an interview invite.

This situation can be confusing, but it does not always mean the interview is cancelled.

---

## Possible Explanations

### 1. The Original Job ID Was Closed

Amazon may close, pause, or update a specific job requisition.

The portal status may refer only to that specific job ID.

You may still be active for:

- Another SDE requisition
- A similar team
- A general SDE hiring pipeline
- A different location
- A recycled interview loop

---

### 2. You May Have Been Moved to Another Role

Sometimes the recruiter may move a candidate from one opening to another if:

- The original role was filled
- The team changed hiring needs
- Another team is a better match
- The candidate passed the assessment and is still eligible for SDE interviews

---

### 3. Portal Status and Recruiter Status May Not Match Immediately

Recruiting systems and candidate portals do not always update in a way that is easy for candidates to understand.

A recruiter email is usually more important than the portal status, but you should confirm directly.

---

### 4. Work Authorization May Require Clarification

If you mentioned F-1 / OPT / STEM OPT, the recruiter may need to check role-specific hiring requirements.

Do not assume rejection unless the recruiter explicitly says the process is cancelled.

---

## What You Should Not Do

Avoid sending emotional messages like:

> Why was I rejected?

Avoid assuming:

- The interview is fake
- You were rejected completely
- Work authorization automatically disqualified you
- Amazon Leo and general SDE are always separate processes

---

## What You Should Do

Send a calm clarification email.

Ask:

1. Is my upcoming SDE interview still active?
2. Is this interview for the same Amazon Leo role or a different SDE requisition?
3. Should I prepare for a general SDE loop or a team-specific loop?
4. Is there anything else needed from my side regarding work authorization?

---

## Best Interpretation

If you received a direct interview invite after the portal changed, treat the interview as active until the recruiter says otherwise.

Prepare seriously.

The safest assumption is:

> The portal status may apply to one job ID, while the interview may still be active under another SDE process.

---

## Candidate Checklist

Before the interview, confirm:

- Interview date and time
- Number of rounds
- Role title
- Team or business unit, if available
- Level, if available
- Location
- Work authorization discussion status
- Whether system design is expected
- Whether the loop is coding-heavy or mixed coding + behavioral
